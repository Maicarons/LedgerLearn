LedgerLearn is a multilingual (Chinese, English, Korean) accounting education app designed specifically for accounting beginners in Chinese Mainland. It teaches double-entry bookkeeping through hands-on simulation — write vouchers, query ledgers, run trial balances, and generate financial statements following China's Accounting Standards for Business Enterprises (ASBE). The chart of accounts, accounting standards, and knowledge content are based on Chinese regulations.

IMPORTANT: This app is tailored for accounting practices of Chinese Mainland.

Features:
• 凭证管理 — 新建/编辑/删除记账凭证，动态分录行，借贷平衡校验，提供 4 种常见业务模板。
• 会计科目 — 59 个预置标准科目（资产/负债/权益/成本/损益），按中国企业会计准则分类，支持自定义科目。
• General & Subsidiary Ledgers — Account-summary view and per-voucher drill-down with Chinese-standard formatting.
• Financial Statements — Trial Balance, Income Statement, and Balance Sheet with educational annotations explaining Chinese accounting principles.
• Knowledge Base — 74 trilingual knowledge cards covering Chinese accounting practice and economic law, rendered in Markdown.
• Export — Export vouchers, ledgers, trial balances, income statements, and balance sheets to CSV or PDF.
• Settings — Real-time language switching (Chinese/English/Korean), light/dark/system theme, and data reset.

Built with Flutter and GetX. Offline-first with local storage — no account registration needed.
